# Auditing solutions in Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 auditing solutions help organizations effectively respond to security events, forensic investigations, compliance obligations, and internal investigations. Thousands of user and admin operations performed in dozens of Microsoft 365 services are captured and recorded in your organization's unified audit log. Audit records for these events are logged and searchable by security operation team, IT teams, insider risk teams, and compliance and legal investigation teams in your organization. 

Enterprise Security Operations Teams, IT teams, compliance, legal/risk Teams

•	Advanced Audit helps me effectively respond to forensic investigations. 
•	Advanced Audit helps me effectively respond to regulatory, compliance and internal obligations


•	Advanced Audit continues to add new events to help customers meet their growing forensic and compliance investigation needs (refer to roadmap). 
•	Crucial events such as Mail Items Accessed event are valuable for post-breach analysis, as it logs each time an email is accessed, read or synced by an intruder.


Insider risks are one of the top concerns of security and compliance professionals in the modern workplace. Industry studies have shown that insider risks are often associated with specific user events or activities. Protecting your organization against these risks can be challenging to identify and difficult to mitigate. Insider risks include vulnerabilities in a variety of areas and can cause major problems for your organization, ranging from the loss of intellectual property to workplace harassment, and more. The following figure outlines common insider risks:

![Insider risk threats](../media/ir-solution-threats.png)

Microsoft 365 risk prevention features are designed and built-in to our insider risk products and solutions. These solutions work together and use advanced service and 3rd-party indicators to help you quickly identify, triage, and act on risk activity. Most solutions offer a comprehensive detection, alert, and remediation workflow for your data analysts and investigators to use to quickly act on and minimize these risks.
