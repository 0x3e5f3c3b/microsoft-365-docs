# Simplified purchase of Microsoft 365 for business

1. Go to the <a href="https://www.aka.ms/office365signup" target="_blank">Microsoft 365 Products site</a>.
2. Select the plan that you want to sign up for, such as **Microsoft 365 Business Standard**.
3. On the next page, follow the steps to set up your account.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> On the **How you'll sign in** page, choose the option to **Create a new business email...**.

1. The sign up process may take several minutes to complete. After it's complete, you're ready to start the setup wizard for your subscription.

## Next steps

1. Once you've completed purchasing setting up your first subscription, follow the steps in the [Set up Microsoft Business Standard](../setup/setup-business-standard.md) topic to get started.
1. Do you have employees or partners that you want to add to your subscription? Follow the steps in the [Set up my business account in Microsoft 365](owner-iw-invite.md) to add them.
