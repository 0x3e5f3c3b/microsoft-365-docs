# Support contacts by country

Billing support is provided in English from 9 AM-5 PM (9 AM-6 PM in Australia), Monday-Friday.\
Technical support is provided in English 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.\
Admins, have your account details ready when you call.

> [!NOTE]
> To better protect your organization, we added a PIN-based verification step to our existing phone-based verification process. If you contact us from a number that isn't registered with your organization profile, the Microsoft support representative sends a verification code to the registered email or phone number in your Microsoft 365 admin center profile. You must provide this code to the support representative to grant them access to your organizationâ€™s account.

- **In the United States, call 1 800 865 9408.**
- **In Australia, call 1 800 197 503.**
- **In Canada, call 1 800 865 9408.**
- **In the United Kingdom, call 0800 032 6417.**
