# Use model discovery in Microsoft SharePoint Syntex

You can make your trained document understanding models available for others to view and select directly from the document library.

Content owners can discover and evaluate trained models, and select the model that is most useful for classifying their files or extracting specific information from them. You can evaluate several models against your content to help you find the most appropriate one.
