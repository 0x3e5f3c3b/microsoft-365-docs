# Step 5: Configure privileged access management for Office 365

*This step is optional and applies only to the E5 version of Microsoft 365 Enterprise*

![](./media/deploy-foundation-infrastructure/infoprotection_icon-small.png)

Privileged access management are settings that specify just-in-time access for task-based activities in your Office 365 tenant. For example, you could configure a privileged access management policy that requires explicit approval to access and change organization mailbox settings in your Office 365 tenant.
