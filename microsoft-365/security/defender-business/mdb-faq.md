# Microsoft Defender for Business - Frequently asked questions and answers

## Why am I not seeing Microsoft Defender for Business in my portal?

Currently when a tenant has multiple licenses - higher SKU take precedence.  
e.g.: P2 + SMB license -> higher SKU take precedence.  

This means that if you are a customer that even has a P2 trial offering enabled on 1 device, we recognize this as P2 and you won’t see the SMB page.
