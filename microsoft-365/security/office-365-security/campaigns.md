Campaigns is a feature in Advanced Threat Protection (ATP) in Office 365 that identifies and categorizes phishing attacks that are detected in Office 365. Campaigns can help you to:

- Efficiently investigate and respond to phishing attacks.

- Better understand the scope of the attack.

- Show value to decision makers.
