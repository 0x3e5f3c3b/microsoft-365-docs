# Tenant management for Microsoft 365 for Enterprise

Definition

## Attributes of a well-designed tenancy


- Network traffic optimized (enterprise)

- Correct set of subscriptions and licenses

- Correct domains configured

- Correct accounts synched and added

  - Accounts mapped to EXO mailboxes with correct domains

  - Accounts assigned appropriate licenses

- Strong identity and access management

  - Secure user sign-in with MFA or passwordless

  - Conditional Access policies

- On-prem Office servers and their data has been migrated to cloud apps

- Device and app management

  - Organization-owned devices enrolled and managed (MDM)

  - Apps for personal devices managed (MAM)

  - Intune device and app policies

## Steps to take

- Determine your tenancies
- Optimize your networking
- Synchronize your identities and enforce secure sign-ins
- Migrate your on-premises Office servers and data
- Deploy device and app management
